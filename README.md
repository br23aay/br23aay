### 🤖 What I Build

I work at the intersection of **reinforcement learning** and **robotics** — training agents to solve real physical tasks, not just games.

My MSc project trained a **Shadow Hand** (24-DoF robotic hand) to rotate a pen 180° using PPO in MuJoCo. The professor said it would take 6–12 months. I did it in 3.

**Results:** 175° rotation achieved · >90% touch sensor accuracy · 3 reproducible runs · Published 2025

📄 Published in IJRES · Vol 13, Issue 6 · Impact Factor 7.52
